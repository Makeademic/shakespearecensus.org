Copy Details for Troilus and Cressida (1609)

SC #: 1556

Location: Victoria and Albert Museum National Art Library

Shelfmark: Dyce 25.D.69

Notes about issue: There are two issues of this edition: in this issue the original title leaf was replaced by a single fold ¶2, which contains a new title page with a different play title and no performance attribution [¶1r], and which adds a new address to readers [¶2r]. [DEEP]
