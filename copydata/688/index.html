Copy Details for The Rape of Lucrece (1632)

SC #: 1175

Location: Oxford University, Corpus Christi College

Shelfmark: phi.B.1.2(1)

Lee #: 26

Lee notes: The Corpus Christi College copy, which measures 5¾ x 3 7/8", was presented to the college by a seventeenth-century Fellow, John Rosewell, Canon of Windsor. It is in old calf, and bound up with a defective copy (having no title) of an English translation by Thomas Hudson of the History of Judith (1584) from the French of Du Bartas.
