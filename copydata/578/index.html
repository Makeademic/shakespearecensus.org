Copy Details for Hamlet (1637)

SC #: 101

Location: Victoria and Albert Museum National Art Library

Shelfmark: Dyce 26 Box 41/2

Provenance notes: Interleaved with MS. collations and notes by Lewis Theobald, to whom this copy once belonged. Donated by Rev. Alexander Dyce.

Bartlett 1939 #: 55

Bartlett 1939 notes: DYCE COLLECTION. 6¾ x 5". With "282" on title: "C.P. A.Dyce" on fly-leaf; "This copy was once the property of that valuable injured Editor, Lewis Theobald. See the collation and Remarks in his handwriting, passim. J. Boaden." on fly-leaf; titles of 1605 and 1611 editions transcribed by Theobald on each side of Smethwicke's device; notes mostly on margin, few on interleaves. The Theobald ( October 23, 1744), Boaden (December 12, 1828), Dyce (bequeathed, 1869) copy. Interleaved and bound in half morocco.
