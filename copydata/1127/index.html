Notes about issue: Most bibliographies list this as a variant issue of Wing S2951 (and date that edition to 1676) but see Emma Depledge, "False Dating: The Case of the '1676' Hamlet Quartos", Papers of the Bibliographical Society of America, 2018. "In this edition, the imprint is in four lines." [ESTC]

Copy-specific notes: "According to the revised 1939 census of Bartlett, only the Harvard copy is larger. This copy is new to bibliography." [ESTC]

Provenance notes: Newly bound in period style calf by Robert Lyon for H.E. Igoe.
