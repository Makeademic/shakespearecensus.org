Copy Details for A Yorkshire Tragedy (1619)

SC #: 1735.5

Location: Folger Shakespeare Library

Shelfmark: V.a.505

Copy-specific notes: Title page only, pasted into a scrapbook made by Halliwell-Phillipps for the Earl of Warwick, containing extracts from early printed books and manuscripts alluding to Shakespeare.

Provenance: Folger, Emily Jordan (1858-1936); Folger, Henry Clay (1857-1930); Halliwell-Phillipps, James Orchard (1820-1889); Warwick, Francis R.C.G. Greville, 5th Earl (1853-1924); Warwick, George Guy Greville, 4th Earl (1818-1893)
